What is CyberPatriot?

"CyberPatriot is the National Youth Cyber Education Program. At the center of CyberPatriot is the National Youth Cyber Defense Competition. The competition puts teams of high school and middle school students in the position of newly hired IT professionals asked with managing the network of a small company. In the rounds of competition teams are given a set of virtual images that represent operating systems and are tasked with finding cybersecurity vulnerabilities within th images and hardening the system while maintaining critical services."

- CyberPatriot Website
